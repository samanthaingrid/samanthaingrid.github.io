THE PLANTS.

Daun Bahagia

10k

Daun Bahagia is easy to care for. It prefers bright, indirect light and should be watered when the top inch of soil dries out.

Sirih Gading Hijau

15k

Sirih Gading thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Water when the top inch of soil is dry, and ensure proper drainage to prevent root rot.

Sukulen Haworthia

21k

Haworthia prefers bright light but not direct sunlight. Plant it in sandy, well-draining soil and water sparingly. Avoid water accumulation in the rosette to prevent rot.
